How to prevent heart disease

control blood pressure

Blood pressure should be in good number, otherwise it causes left ventricle to thicken which leads to heart disease.

Maintain Cholesterol

There are two types of cholesterol, HDL is good for your heart whereas LDL is bad for your heart.

Healthy Diet

Maintaining healthy diet is important as it looks after your health, improves your health.

Limit Alchol and Don't smoke

Drinking alcohol or smoking raises your blood pressure which is not good for your body and heart.

Exercise Reguraly

Regular exercise helps to maintain body. It includes strengthening your heart and improves health.

Manage Stress

Stress is linked to heart disease in many ways. Extreme stress can cause higher risk of heart Disease.

Tests to detect heart disease

Blood Tests

When your muscle has been damaged, from heart disease it releases substances in your body. The relased substance can be measured and knows how much it is damaged.

Electocardiogram(ECG)

ECG helps to read the heart's electrical impulses and shows how well your heart is beating.

Exercise stress test

This test is similar to ECG, where this test helps to detect how well the heart is functioning when exercising.

MRI(Magnetic Resonance Imaging)

This test helps to detect the functioning of heart and also helps to detect how much damage has been done.
